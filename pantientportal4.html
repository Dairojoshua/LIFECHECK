About

Born 1993, Adebayo Dorherty Orekoya is the third in a family of seven children, indigene of Odiolowo township in Oshogbo Local Government Area of Osun State. Nigeria.

For about 10 years now, I have been living with multiple sclerosis (MS). This requires significant lifestyle modifications for me to accommodate the physical limitations imposed by the health condition.

I remain proactive in my treatment plan, and able to adapt to this new reality marked by unpredictable symptoms and the need for ongoing medical care.

I constantly struggle with anxiety, depression and the psychological impact of adjusting to life with a chronic disease. Despite the challenges, I pursue my personal and career goal diligently

I quite appreciate the encouragement and understanding received by my family members as I journey in this critical health condition
